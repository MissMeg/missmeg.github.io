About

Hi! My name is Megan and I'm a developer. I have had an interesting path to get where I am today, so let's start at the beginning...

After earning my bachelor's degree, I decided to take a break before graduate school and choose to move abroad and teach English. Living in Shanghai, China was a lot of fun and I learned a lot about myself and the Chinese culture. (There's nothing quite like trying to communicate complicated topics in a language you only know basic words in!) Teaching in Shanghai was a wonderful experience and led me to earning a master's degree in Education.

I spent three years teaching in Shanghai before deciding to move back to the U.S. (I really missed my family). I found a job teaching, but learned quickly there are huge differences between teaching internationally and teaching in the U.S. I started to think about what other opportunities where out there that gave me daily challenges and puzzles like teaching did and I found my way back to programming.

I started learning Front-End Web Development and found a lot of joy and excitement while working on personal projects. The next step was earning my Techdegree in Full Stack JavaScript in order to grow my portfolio, as well as, to continue to learn and practice new skills. Now, I continue to learn on my own and I have taken on learning Python as well.
